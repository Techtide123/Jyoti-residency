Welcome to Hotel Jyoti Residency Hotel

Hotel Jyoti Residency Hotel

Welcome to Hotel Jyoti Residency, a charming retreat located opposite the Old Bus Stand in Berhampur, Ganjam, Odisha. Established on 1st July 2017, our hotel offers a serene and inviting ambiance, making it the perfect choice for both business and leisure travelers. Experience the comfort of our well-furnished rooms, featuring elegant décor and modern amenities designed to ensure a pleasant and relaxing stay. Guests can choose from a variety of room types and rate plans, including the Presidential Suite and Deluxe Room, each offering a unique blend of comfort and sophistication. Enjoy a peaceful night’s rest, savor delicious meals at our on-site restaurant, and experience the warmth of genuine hospitality. Conveniently located near key attractions and business centers, Hotel Jyoti Residency promises exceptional service, comfort, and convenience for an unforgettable stay in Berhampur.
