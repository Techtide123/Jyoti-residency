Weddings

Hotel Jyoti Residency is a popular choice for weddings, offering both indoor and outdoor spaces to accommodate large gatherings. They provide facilities like in-house and outside catering, decoration services, and DJ services.

Birthday Parties

The hotel is well-suited for birthday parties, with different halls available for various group sizes. They offer vegetarian cuisine and have rooms available for guests to stay overnight.

Corporate Events

The hotel can host corporate events such as conferences, meetings, and seminars. They provide air-conditioned halls, parking facilities, and other amenities to ensure a successful event.
