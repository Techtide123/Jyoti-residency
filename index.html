Welcome to Hotel Jyoti Residency Hotel

Welcome to Hotel Jyoti Residency , a delightful haven nestled in the heart of Kanpur. Our hotel offers a serene and inviting ambiance, perfect for both business and leisure travelers. Immerse yourself in the comfort of our well-appointed rooms, featuring elegant decor and modern amenities. Indulge in a restful night's sleep on our plush beds, ensuring a rejuvenating stay. Wake up to a complimentary breakfast and savor a variety of delectable dishes at our on-site restaurant. Conveniently located near major attractions and business centers, Hotel Jyoti Residency provides easy access to the best of Kanpur. Experience warm hospitality and impeccable service throughout your stay.
